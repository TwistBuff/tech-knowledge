What is tech ?

Technology, often abbreviated as "tech," refers to the practical application of scientific knowledge to create tools, systems, and products that solve problems and improve our lives. It spans various fields, including information technology, computer science, electronics, artificial intelligence, and more. From everyday gadgets to cutting-edge innovations, technology is constantly evolving, shaping the way we communicate, work, and play. It's at the heart of progress and drives our modern society forward.

Difference between IT and CS

Information Technology (IT)

  • Practical implementation and management of computer systems.
  • Focus on computer networks, hardware, software, and databases.
  • Roles include network administrators, system analysts, technical support specialists, and database administrators.
  • Ensures efficient and secure technology use for organizations.

Computer Science (CS)

  • Theoretical foundations and scientific principles of computation.
  • Involves programming languages, algorithms, software design, and research.
  • Roles span software development, artificial intelligence, data science, etc.
  • Drives innovation and explores new technologies and solutions.
